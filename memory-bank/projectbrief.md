# Project Brief: Al-Sufiaan School Management System

## Project Overview
Al-Sufiaan School Management System is a comprehensive web-based application designed to digitize and streamline the administrative operations of Al-Sufiaan School. The system provides a complete solution for managing school operations including staff management, financial tracking, inventory management, fee collection, and vendor management.

## Core Requirements

### Primary Objectives
1. **Staff Management**: Complete CRUD operations for teaching and non-teaching staff with photo management
2. **Financial Management**: Track expenses, manage vendor bills and payments, handle fee categories and pricing
3. **Inventory Management**: Product management with transaction tracking and sales functionality
4. **Fee Management**: Configure fee categories, class-wise pricing, and transportation area pricing
5. **Vendor Management**: Comprehensive vendor relationship management with billing and payment tracking
6. **Authentication & Authorization**: Secure user authentication with role-based access control

### Key Features
- **Public Website**: Landing page, about, team, gallery, contact, and fees information
- **Admin Dashboard**: Centralized management interface for all school operations
- **Staff Management**: Add, view, edit teaching and non-teaching staff with photo uploads
- **Expense Tracking**: Categorized expense management with vendor integration
- **Inventory System**: Product management with sales tracking and transaction history
- **Fee Structure**: Flexible fee category and pricing management
- **Transportation**: Area-wise transportation pricing management

## Technical Scope
- **Frontend**: React-based SPA with TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and modern UI components
- **Backend**: Node.js/Express API with TypeScript, MySQL database, and Cloudinary integration
- **Authentication**: JWT-based authentication with cookie management
- **File Management**: Cloudinary integration for photo uploads and management
- **Database**: MySQL with Sequelize ORM for data modeling and relationships

## Success Criteria
1. Complete digitization of school administrative processes
2. Secure and efficient staff data management
3. Accurate financial tracking and reporting
4. Streamlined inventory and sales operations
5. Flexible fee management system
6. User-friendly interface for administrators
7. Reliable data persistence and backup
8. Scalable architecture for future enhancements

## Constraints
- Must maintain data security and privacy
- Should be accessible via web browsers
- Must handle concurrent users efficiently
- Should provide real-time data updates
- Must integrate with existing school workflows
